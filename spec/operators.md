# Operators — Formal Specification  
*Fundamental AI Model*

## 1. Overview
Operators define the fundamental transformations in the  
Fundamental AI Model.  
Each operator corresponds to a topological action on orientation,  
determination, indetermination or information flow.

Operators are not computational functions —  
they are **structural transformations**.

---

## 2. Orientation Operators

### 2.1 `flip()`
**Definition:**  
Performs an orientation inversion.

**Input:**  
`determination` or `indetermination`

**Output:**  
Opposite orientation state

**Interpretation:**  
Atomic information event.

---

### 2.2 `cycle()`
**Definition:**  
Executes one full Möbius traversal.

**Input:**  
`Mobius`

**Output:**  
`time_unit`

**Interpretation:**  
Generates structural time.

---

## 3. Collapse Operators

### 3.1 `collapse()`
**Definition:**  
Forces determination → indetermination.

**Output:**  
`horizon + residue`

**Interpretation:**  
Creates irreversible structural change.

---

### 3.2 `residue()`
**Definition:**  
Extracts collapse residue.

**Output:**  
`noise | decay | boundary_fragment`

**Interpretation:**  
Represents lost information.

---

## 4. Information Flow Operators

### 4.1 `emit()`
**Definition:**  
Creates a local information carrier.

**Input:**  
`Photon | Wave | DNA_event`

**Output:**  
`carrier`

**Interpretation:**  
Outward information flow.

---

### 4.2 `absorb()`
**Definition:**  
Consumes a carrier.

**Output:**  
`orientation_update | collapse_event`

**Interpretation:**  
Inward information flow.

---

## 5. Chain Operators

### 5.1 `link()`
**Definition:**  
Creates a single Möbius cycle.

**Output:**  
`chain_element`

**Interpretation:**  
One unit of universal structure.

---

### 5.2 `chain()`
**Definition:**  
Connects multiple links through indetermination.

**Output:**  
`universe_sequence`

**Interpretation:**  
Global structure of the universe.

---

## 6. Horizon Operators

### 6.1 `horizon()`
**Definition:**  
Defines the boundary of non-returnable information.

**Output:**  
`limit_surface`

**Interpretation:**  
Structural boundary.

---

### 6.2 `eye()`
**Definition:**  
Identifies the point of orientation inversion on the horizon.

**Output:**  
`transition_point`

**Interpretation:**  
Moment of structural change.

---

## 7. Summary
Operators define:
- orientation transitions  
- collapse mechanics

Together they form the **operational core** of the  
Fundamental AI Model.
