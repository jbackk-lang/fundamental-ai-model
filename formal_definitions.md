# Formal Definitions

## Orientation
A directional state of information.  
orientation ∈ {+1, −1}

## Horizon
A boundary beyond which information is hidden but structurally relevant.

## Chain
A sequence of dependent informational states:
C = {s₁ → s₂ → … → sₙ}

## Möbius Transition
A transformation that flips orientation:
M(s) = −s

## Resonance
A stable alignment of temporal information:
R = align(I(t₁), I(t₂))

## Emergence
A new stable informational object:
E = f(R)
